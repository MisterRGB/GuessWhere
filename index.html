Globe Guess

This game is designed to test your knowledge of geography. It itentionally has no boundaries lines to challenge your knowledge of countries and their locations.

How to Play:

A country name will be displayed. Click anywhere on the globe to place your pin where you think that country is located.

Click "Confirm Guess" to lock in your answer. The closer you are, the higher your score! The timer also affects your score, so guess quickly!

Use the mouse/touch to rotate and zoom the globe.
